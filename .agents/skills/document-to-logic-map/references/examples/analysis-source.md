# Pricing pressure analysis

## Core claim
SMB churn is rising because price sensitivity increased faster than perceived product value.

## Evidence
- In the last 2 quarters, churn in the SMB segment rose from 4.8% to 7.1%.
- Win-loss interviews repeatedly mention “hard to justify renewal”.
- Competitors now bundle reporting features in lower tiers.

## Risks
- We may over-attribute churn to price and ignore onboarding issues.

## Questions
- Which churned accounts had low feature adoption before cancellation?

## Metrics
- Renewal rate by segment
- Feature adoption rate before churn
